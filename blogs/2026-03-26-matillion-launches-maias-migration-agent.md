Matillion announced the public preview of Migration Agent, a new AI capability that autonomously converts legacy ETL pipelines from 14 platforms into native, warehouse-optimized pipelines on modern cloud data warehouses. The tool aims to eliminate expensive consulting engagements and manual rewrites, compressing multi-quarter migration programs into weeks.
